# PhenoSage v2 - Project Brief

PhenoSage v2 is a professional cannabis cultivation AI operating system for serious growers.

The product is not a generic grow journal. It is an AI-native grow operations platform where a user can create grows, add plants, upload images/videos, receive structured AI plant-health analysis, track plant progression over time, receive proactive findings/tasks/alerts, and chat with a grow-aware AI assistant.

## Core Pillars

1. **Visual Grow Doctor:** Image/video based diagnosis of plant health.
2. **Longitudinal Plant Intelligence:** Comparison of captures over time to identify trends.
3. **Grow-aware AI Copilot:** Context-sensitive chat based on actual grow data.
4. **Proactive Findings, Tasks, and Alerts:** Severity-ranked notifications and automation.
5. **Media-first Grow Memory:** High-fidelity audit trail of every observation.

## Primary User

Professional or serious cannabis cultivator managing one or more grows.

## Scope Boundary

**Cultivation only.** Do not provide medical, legal, dosing, dispensary, recreational-use, or sales advice. The system must be technically credible, privacy-preserving, secure, and production-oriented.
